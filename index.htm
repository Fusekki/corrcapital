What Is CORR Jewelry?

CORR JEWELRY is a full-service concierge jewelry service available exclusively to American Expres Centurion Cardholders and select clientele of our CORR Hotels properties. From elegant bespok creations to private collection management, CORR Jewelry provides an unrivaled level of service sophistication and access for a privileged and discerning few.

    Crisis
    Management

    We offer our clients an elite level of personalized service. Where the situation dictates, members of our team can be by your side at a moment’s notice, to guide you through the process of stopping identity theft as it happens. Experience has taught us that it is most productive to meet with victims of identity theft at a location where they are best able to best access their financial information, account statements, etc. Therefore, we often recommend meeting at our client’s homes or places of business, so that their personal information will be accessible as we begin the process of making all of the necessary notifications and requests. Additionally, we accompany our clients to their local police precinct to file a police report, which includes and FTC Affidavit. Although it may be time consuming, it is imperative to file a police report as soon as possible after identity theft has been detected, in order to limit future liability and exposure.
