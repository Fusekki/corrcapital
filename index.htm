CORR Benefits

HEALTHCARE COST MANAGEMENT SOLUTIONS

OVERVIEW

CORR Benefits (“CORR”) and its strategic partners provide public and private employers with concept-proven, non-disruptive, cost containment strategies that maximize savings and shareholder value, by design. Our partners and us represent more than 3,000 companies, all 50 US states, 30 million lives and over $30 billion USD in annual drug spend. Our proven solutions are wholly non-disruptive, seamlessly integrated into existing platforms and require no changes to existing vendor relationships. Our clients – which include Fortune 100 corporations, state and local government agencies (including self-insured and self-funded) organizations) – assume no financial risk while reaping the corporate responsibility benefits of a healthier workforce, and a healthier America.

Moses Gross

Managing Director

MOSES GROSS, President and CEO of ANM Group | ANM Management | ANM Real Estate | MG Builders Watermark Center, Watermark Lofts, Watermark 920 and Watermark Studios | Watermark Group (a Publicly traded company). Moses Gross is the team leader of a solidly successful commercial and residential real estate development and a syndicate management firm. As an exceptionally talented investor, Moses researches potential markets to identify demands and trends, analyzes growth possibilities, and combines his firm commitment and excellent rapport of his associates and clientele for continued optimum success. With years of specialty real estate experience, prior of which he competently advised various significant corporate companies on investment and fiscal strategy and administrations. His ability to recognize and accurately distinguish strategic environments, aggressively launch and achieve results, enthusiastic, charismatic and dependable personality and continuously successful performance record have established Moses as a vibrant fundamental power in any business opportunities. Moses has consistently and expertly promoted the closing of over 500 various use investment closings. Mr. Gross founded ANM Real Estate and ANM Management in 1997 and completed his first development in 1999. In the last 18 years, ANM Group has prospered into a full service integrated real estate firm and has developed more than 50 properties totaling over 5 million square feet of property and over $200 million in project developments.

David Redlener

Managing Director

David is a licensed attorney with a background in commercial lending and public relations. David began his career as an Assistant District Attorney in the Bronx County District Attorney’s Office. In 1997, David began a decade-long role as the Senior Vice President and Deputy Director of Loan Services at one of the largest non -bank small business financing companies in the nation. In this capacity, David played a critical role in supporting the firm’s portfolio growth from under $70 million to an excess of $2.7 billion. Prior to joining Watermark, David had leveraged his legal, commercial lending and communications expertise to provide public relations and communications consulting services while at DKC, a Public Relations Consulting and Management firm, ranked #1 on The Observer’s 2015 PR Power firms in NYC, and through his own firm, CMJ Advisory Consultants.

Nick Stefanov

Managing Director

Nicholas Stefanov is an American investor and fund-manager. As President of Watermark Capital, Nick brings his experience in deal development and execution to the team in Watermark’s endeavor to develop a portfolio of highly valuable real estate assets. Prior to Watermark, Nick was managing partner at International Electric Power. IEP’s mission was to develop Green and Sustainable power production in emerging markets. Deal sizes for this fund were between $200M - $600M. Before IEP, Nick was a Principal and Managing Partner at the Barish Fund, a middle-market private Equity fund. Investment focus at this fund was on healthcare, business to business solutions, manufacturing and consumer products. Deal sizes were typically between $50M-$150M. Nick has shepherded his Venture-Capital holding company, NIS holdings through the development and early round funding and mentorship of such companies such as the tech giant, Terremark, which was recently sold to Verizon for total transaction value in excess of $2B and the creation of The Light Group; one of the largest hospitality companies in Las Vegas and abroad. Nick sits on the boards of several businesses as well as those of City Government.
